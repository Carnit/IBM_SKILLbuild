Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts

The impacts of the climate crisis are profound and far-reaching. Rising temperatures contribute to more frequent and severe weather events, such as hurricanes, heatwaves, and droughts. Melting ice caps and glaciers are causing sea levels to rise, threatening coastal communities and ecosystems. Ocean acidification, a direct result of increased carbon dioxide absorption, is disrupting marine life and coral reefs.
